# ムーラ・バンダ（mūla-bandha）

## 区分
**区分A — HYP確認済み**（HYP 第3章「The Mula Bandha」）

## 読み
ムーラ・バンダ

## 語源
mūla（ムーラ / 根・基底）＋ bandha（バンダ / 締め・錠）。
直訳は「根の締め」。会陰部（mūlādhāra）を収縮させることに由来する。

## 原典記述（HYP 第3章）

> Pressing Yoni (perineum) with the heel, contract up the anus. By drawing the Apana thus, Mula Bandha is made.

> The Apana, naturally inclining downward, is made to go up by force. This Mula Bandha is spoken of by Yogis and Yoginis as done by contracting the anus.

> Pressing the heel well against the anus, draw up the air by force, again and again till it (air) goes up.

> Prana, Apana, Nada and Bindu uniting into one in this way, give success in Yoga, undoubtedly.

> By the purification of Prana, and Apana, urine and excrements decrease. Even an old man or woman becomes young by constantly practicing Mula Bandha.

> The Kundalini, which has been sleeping all this time, becomes well heated by this means and awakens well. It becomes straight like a serpent, struck dead with a stick.

> It enters the Brahma Nadi, just like a serpent enters its hole. Therefore, the Yogi or Yogini should always practice this Mula Bandha.

## 解説
踵で会陰（ヨーニ）を押さえ肛門を上方に収縮させる根本錠。アパーナ・ヴァーユ（下行気）を上昇させ、プラーナ・アパーナ・ナーダ・ビンドゥを一体化させる。
クンダリニーを加熱・覚醒させブラフマ・ナーディー（スシュムナー）へ蛇のように入らせるとされる。
常時修習することが推奨される。
